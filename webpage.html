Renzo Mendoza Rabanal


I am a BSc graduate in Mechatronics Engineering from the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru (PUCP), currently working as a Research Engineer for the Brain-body Interaction Lab at Harvard University. My specialization lies in Control Theory, Dynamical Systems and Computational Neuroscience. I aspire to contribute to the research in developing mathematical or computational representations that describe the complex brain-body feedback system.
